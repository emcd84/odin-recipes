Chicken Fingers

Chicken fingers with crunchy coating. Great with BBQ sauce and ketchup.

Ingredients:

  • Skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into strips
  • Eggs, beaten
  • Milk
  • Tortilla chips, crushed

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Mix egg beat and mix in bowl. Place crushed chips in separate bowl. Dip chicken first in egg/milk mixture, then in crushed chips to coat. Place on ungreased cookie sheet.
  3. Bake for ten minutes, flip, bake for ten additional minutes.
