French Fries!

Chicken Fingers

Crunchy, delicious fries made in your oven!

Ingredients:

  • Baking potatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • White sugar
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Line baking sheet with foil and spray. Cut potatoes into half inch thick fries.
  2. Toss potatoes with oil, sugar, salt, and red pepper. Spread on baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 30 minutes in preheated oven until potatoes are tender and brown.
