Beef Tacos

Beef tacos for those who enjoy good food!

Ingredients:

  • Taco shells
  • Ground beef
  • Taco kit
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 375 degrees.
  2. Warm taco shells for five minutes.
  3. In a skillet, brown the beef. Halfway through browning, add spices.
  4. Spoon the meat into the taco shells and top as you choose.
